About The Automan's Daughter

The Automan's Daughter is an action-adventure webcomic set in a world of steam and steel. Follow Aisha as she navigates a dangerous landscape of mechanical wonders, political intrigue, and personal discovery.

This comic explores themes of identity, family, and what it means to be human in a world where the line between man and machine grows ever thinner.
